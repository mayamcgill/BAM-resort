Location

Atlantis is a beautiful underwater resort off the coast of California. With flora and fauna unlike any other resort, Atlantis is a truly unique destination, ready for you to dive right in. Whether you're bringing family, a special someone, or you're exploring solo, our aquatic experience is one to amaze.

Who can enjoy Atlantis?

All are welcome at Atlantis, making it a truly magical society. That being said, there are two main Atlantis experiences. These are the family experience and the forever young experience. The forever young experience is the primary journey, targeted at young adults. It includes tours, wine tastings, and discounted rentals for diving. The family experience has affordable offers for babysitting and a discount for various rides. Our facilities are mainly targeted at the young adult visitors.
